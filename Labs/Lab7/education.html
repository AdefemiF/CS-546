All about Femi's education

So you wanna know where I went to school, huh? Let's get into it!

CornerStone Christian School

My favorite Class at cornerstone, was the music class! I loved playing the recorder because it led to me playing the flute, which led to the drums, and now I'm a music producer!

One of my favorite memories was going to the liberty science center! It was so cool being able to see all the new technologies and seeing the IMAX theater!

Parsons Elementary School

My favorite class here was 100% band! I loved being able to play instruments as it was the one thing I felt like I was really good at.

Can't remember too much but I remember making TieDie Shirts for our yearly fun day! That was super fun and I still remember it so vividly!

Linwood Middle School

My favorite class here was 100% band. I got to play the drums and it's where I learned to play the drumset!

One of my favorite memories was our send off when I was in the 8th grade. It was crazy to see all my friends have fun together at night!

North Brunswick TownShip High School

Favorite class had to have been percussion ensemble. It's where I learned to hone in my drum skills and where I joined the school drumline for competitions!

I think my send off in the day before we graduated was my favorite memory. Seeing everyone happy and smilin was a sight I'll never forget, especially now that we're all adults.

Stevens Institute of Technology

My favorite class at stevens? Barely any but I'd probably say Failure. Yes, there's a class on Failure at stevens, ironic right?

Favorite memories were made during the summer of 2018 during the STEP program. It was absolutely the most fun I've ever had in a single summer!
